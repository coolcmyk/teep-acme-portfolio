# Mie-Grüneisen EOS

An alternative equation of state available in the MPM framework, based on the Mie-Grüneisen relation.

### Basic Concept
Relates pressure, energy, and volume through the Grüneisen parameter Γ:
p − p_ref(V) = (Γ/V)·(E − E_ref(V))

Where p_ref and E_ref define a reference curve (typically the Hugoniot).

### In This Paper
- Included as one of the EOS options in the MPM code
- Not the primary EOS — Tillotson is the main choice
- Available for future studies or material-specific applications
- Other EOS options: elastic relation, linear polynomial, simplified Tillotson
